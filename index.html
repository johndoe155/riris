Our Heritage

A Story Written
in Excellence

Since 1995, Bodija International College has been shaping minds, building character, and transforming futures — one student at a time.

1995 — Founding

The Vision Takes Root

Founded in the heart of Bodija, Ibadan, Bodija International College opened its gates with a bold ambition: to bridge global standards of education with the rich intellectual tradition of Nigeria.

2001 — First Class

A Generation Graduates

Our inaugural graduating class achieved remarkable distinction results, validating the rigorous standards our founders had set and establishing a benchmark of academic excellence that endures today.

2008 — Expansion

A Campus Transformed

State-of-the-art science laboratories, a comprehensive research library, and new sports facilities were commissioned — investing in the physical environment that inspires great minds.

2015 — Accreditation

Internationally Recognised

BIC received formal accreditation for its curriculum, cementing its reputation as one of Oyo State's most distinguished secondary institutions and opening doors for students to world-class universities.

2020 — Digital Era

Education Reimagined

Pioneering the integration of digital tools, BIC ensured students remained at the frontier of modern learning — adapting and excelling when the world demanded resilience and innovation most.

Today — A Living Legacy

The Story Continues

Today, BIC continues to produce leaders, innovators, and change-makers — carrying our founding motto "Success Through Labour" into every classroom, every competition, and every life we touch.
